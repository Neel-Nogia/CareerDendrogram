About Career Dendrogram

Problem ID: PID139 - Challenge Title: Career Dendrogram

Tribal Development Department Initiative

Our Mission

Career Dendrogram is designed to address the critical need for career guidance among students. Every student has unique aspirations and talents, but often lacks the proper guidance to make informed decisions about their future.

We provide comprehensive, authentic information about career paths, educational institutes, job trends, and future opportunities to help students select the right course and excel in their chosen field.

The Problem We Solve

Lack of Guidance

Students face confusion in choosing career paths due to limited access to career counseling and information.

Wrong Decisions

Without proper guidance, students often make incorrect course selections leading to dissatisfaction and career changes.

Information Gap

Limited knowledge about available institutes, courses, and their specializations hinders informed decision-making.

Our Solution

College Database

Comprehensive information about top institutes with details on programs, fees, contact information, and specializations.

Career Explorer

Detailed career path information including salary ranges, job outlook, required skills, and education requirements.

AI Assistant

24/7 intelligent chatbot providing instant answers to career queries and personalized guidance.

Job Market Trends

Up-to-date information on job market trends, salary expectations, and future career prospects.

Target Users

  • Students completing 10th and 12th grade
  • Students seeking career guidance at early stages
  • Parents looking for authentic career information for their children
  • Career counselors and educators
  • Anyone exploring career change opportunities
